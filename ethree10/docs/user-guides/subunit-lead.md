# Sub-unit lead guide

You assign work to members in your sub-unit and review their completions.

## Assigning work
1. Open a project and choose **Add task**.
2. Pick your sub-unit, then use the **Assignee picker** — it shows each member's open-task count and skills so you can route to the best fit.
3. Set priority, estimate, and due date.

## Reviewing completions
- When a member submits a completion, the task moves to **In review** and you're notified.
- Open the task and either **Accept** (it moves to Done) or **Request changes** (it returns to the member with your note).

## Keeping an eye on load
- **Members** shows everyone's current open-task count — watch for anyone overloaded and rebalance.

## Reports
- **Reports → My week** and the sub-unit reports summarize completions and on-time rate. Generate the weekly rollup to refresh them.
