# Team head guide

You own delivery quality and throughput for one of Ethree10's delivery teams: Product Development or Brands & Communications.

## Daily operating rhythm

1. Open **Team dashboard** to review intake, workload, overdue work, and review queues.
2. Open **Team intake** for requests routed to your team.
3. Assign work from **Team assignments** or the request/project detail page.
4. Review submitted deliverables in **Team reviews** before the client is notified.
5. Use **Team workload** before assigning more work to someone already carrying too much.

## Intake and routing

- **Inbox** is the agency-wide intake surface for new submitted requests.
- Requests with a configured service can route directly to your team.
- Cross-team or unclear requests stay in agency review until an agency admin selects the right team.
- If the request needs more information, move it to clarification rather than creating vague tasks.

## Delivery control

- Every accepted request should become a project with clear tasks, owners, review requirements, and acceptance criteria.
- Team members submit completion evidence before work is marked done.
- You should vet the work, request changes if needed, then approve delivery.
- Public client acceptance happens through the secure tracking link; clients do not need internal accounts.

## Reporting

- Use **Team reports** for team output, quality, timeliness, and review history.
- Individual effort is tracked through task completions, time logs, contributions, and review outcomes.
- Weekly and monthly reports should mention both team progress and individual contributions.

## Commercial handoff

- Finance or agency leadership owns invoices and receipts.
- Team heads should confirm delivery evidence and scope completion before an invoice is treated as final.
