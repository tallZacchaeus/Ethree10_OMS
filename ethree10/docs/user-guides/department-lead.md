# Department lead guide

You own your department's outcomes: triage routed requests, shape sub-units, and watch delivery.

## Triage and routing
- **Inbox** shows incoming requests across all client workspaces awaiting triage.
- Open a request and use the **Triage** panel to route it to a department and move it through its lifecycle.
- Approving a request automatically creates the **Project** that holds the work.

## Structure
- **Departments** lets you create and rename sub-units as the team grows.

## Delivery
- **Projects** shows in-flight work. Open a project to see the task board (Kanban) and mark it delivered when done.

## Billing
- **Invoices → New invoice** raises an invoice against a project. Open it to see
  the full detail page; it starts as a **draft**.
- **Mark sent** renders the branded PDF, stores it, and exposes the public link
  clients pay on. Use **Regenerate PDF** if you change details, or **Void** to
  cancel an invoice that won't be paid.
- **Online:** clients pay NGN invoices via **Paystack** on the public page; the
  webhook flips the invoice to **Paid** and auto-issues exactly one **receipt**.
- **Offline:** for a bank transfer, cheque, or cash payment, open the invoice and
  use **Record payment** — pick the method, add the reference (e.g. the cheque
  number or transfer ref), and confirm. That marks the invoice **Paid** and issues
  the same auto-receipt. Record once the funds have cleared; if a cheque later
  bounces, **Void** the invoice to reverse it.
- Every receipt has its own shareable public link, listed under **Receipts**.

## Reporting
- **Reports** rolls up member and sub-unit performance weekly. Use **Generate weekly** to refresh, and review on-time rates before management check-ins.

## Integrations
- **Integrations** is where you connect Plane (or other tools) so your department's tasks mirror into your execution environment.
