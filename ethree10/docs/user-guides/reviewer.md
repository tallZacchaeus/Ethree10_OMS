# Reviewer guide

You verify submitted work before it is counted as delivered.

## What to review

- The task summary explains what changed.
- Evidence links or attachments are present.
- Acceptance criteria are satisfied.
- Required specialist reviews are complete.
- The work is suitable for client visibility if the project is about to be delivered.

## Review outcomes

- **Approve** when the work meets the brief and quality bar.
- **Request changes** when the work is incomplete, unclear, or below quality expectations.
- Add a specific note when requesting changes so the assignee can act without another meeting.

## Reporting impact

Reviews affect both team quality signals and individual contribution records. Keep review notes factual and tied to the brief, not personal preference.
