# Department lead guide

You own your department's outcomes: triage routed requests, shape sub-units, and watch delivery.

## Triage and routing
- **Inbox** shows incoming requests across all client workspaces awaiting triage.
- Open a request and use the **Triage** panel to route it to a department and move it through its lifecycle.
- Approving a request automatically creates the **Project** that holds the work.

## Structure
- **Departments** lets you create and rename sub-units as the team grows.

## Delivery
- **Projects** shows in-flight work. Open a project to see the task board (Kanban) and mark it delivered when done.

## Reporting
- **Reports** rolls up member and sub-unit performance weekly. Use **Generate weekly** to refresh, and review on-time rates before management check-ins.

## Integrations
- **Integrations** is where you connect Plane (or other tools) so your department's tasks mirror into your execution environment.
